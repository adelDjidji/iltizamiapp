Iltizami إلتزامي

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Iltizami (إلتزامي) is an Islamic accountability and self-reflection app designed to help Muslims track their daily prayers, worship, and spiritual goals. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

All personal data you enter in Iltizami is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate a backend server and do not transmit your personal accountability records to any external service.

2. Data We Collect

The table below summarises every category of data the app handles:

Data type What it includes Where it is stored
Location Device GPS coordinates and inferred city/district name used to calculate accurate prayer times. On-device (Redux Persist / AsyncStorage). Never uploaded.
Accountability records Daily scores for prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, Sunnah, Tahajjud), Quran recitation, fasting, charity, and character traits. On-device only. Never shared.
40-Day challenge Daily completion status, streaks, and star count for the congregation prayer challenge. On-device only.
Goals User-created spiritual goals and their completion status. On-device only.
Notification settings Per-prayer notification preferences and delay settings. On-device only.
App preferences Language (Arabic / English) and theme (Light / Dark) preferences. On-device only.
Diagnostic data Crash reports and anonymous error logs collected by Sentry to help us identify bugs. Sentry servers. No personally identifiable information is included.

3. Device Permissions

  • Location (Fine): Required to fetch accurate prayer times for your city. You may also enter your location manually through the location picker, in which case GPS access is not needed.
  • Notifications: Used to deliver prayer-time reminders that you configure. You can disable any or all reminders from the app settings at any time.

Both permissions are requested only when needed and can be revoked at any time through your device's system settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Iltizami uses the following external services:

  • Aladhan Prayer Times API (api.aladhan.com) — receives your coordinates (latitude and longitude) to return the five daily prayer times. No account or identifier is created; only your coordinates are sent for each request.
  • Sentry — collects anonymous crash and error reports to help us improve app stability. Reports do not include personal accountability data or identifiable information.
  • Expo Updates — used to check for and deliver over-the-air app updates. This service may collect basic device metadata as described in Expo's own privacy policy.

5. How We Use Your Data

  • To display accurate prayer times based on your location.
  • To store and display your personal accountability records and statistics.
  • To send prayer-time notifications according to your preferences.
  • To track your progress in the 40-day challenge and goal list.
  • To improve app stability via anonymous crash reporting.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party beyond what is described in Section 4. Your daily accountability records, challenge history, and spiritual goals never leave your device.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. You can reset your accountability history at any time from the Statistics screen within the app. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

Iltizami is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children beyond what is described in this policy, and all such data remains on-device.

9. Security

Your data is stored locally using AsyncStorage on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, device encryption). We do not control the security of your device; we recommend keeping your operating system and the app up to date.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be made available within the app and on our distribution page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
